ABOUT MYSELF

Hey! My name is Sven and I studied industrial engineering in Munich. After graduating in 2011 I started to work as a quality engineer in the automotive industry. During my studies and worklife I always had a strong interest in learning programming and web-development. The idea of creating a digital product by combining design and coding skills was always fascinating to me.
After 8 years trying to improve car-quality I finaly decided to make a change. I quit my job and went traveling. I went hiking in the Andes and learned spanish in Peru. After that I visited China and spent about 3 month in a shaolin monestary to learn and practice kung-fu. This time off has helped me to make it clearer where I should go in the future.
When I came back this year I started to search for opportunities to follow the path as a software-/web-developer. So I attended a coding bootcamp to learn how to code and build some awesome projects. Now I'm looking for a new opportunity to learn and grow further as a software-/web-developer and I hope we can work together and create something great!
